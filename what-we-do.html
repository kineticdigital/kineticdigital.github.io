Working with Us

We work with you to create solutions that drive brand and business momentum.

We embrace your process and make it our process

Our team works to understand and adapt to your process, allowing your team to focus on the work not learning new processes. We can adapt to most any process from daily stand-ups, to remote sessions with multiple locations, or whatever is most direct to inform stakeholders and provide needed input for the project team.

We integrate seamlessly into your team

Our approach is to organize a project around the needs of our clients. That means we can do it all, or they can supply talent such as Project Management and we will fill in the gaps. It also means clients have access to any team member involved in the project, including developers.

We use popular tools to keep things on track

We use tools like JIRA, Basecamp, Toggl, and GIThub to keep things on track. This ensures that everyone has access to needed information about the project anytime from anywhere. We can also handle things in a more traditional manner i.e. face-2-face, regular phone meetings and email.
